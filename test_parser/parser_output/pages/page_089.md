# BACHELOR OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION IN ACCOUNTING - Study Plan - Abu Dhabi

| BUS 204 | Business Research Methods                      | 3      | STT 100 + BUS 102                                                                           |
| ECO 201 | Principles of Microeconomics                   | 3      | ENG 200 + (MTB101/MTT101/MTT102)                                                            |
