# Workplace Stress and Its Relationship to Mental Health

The strategies for handling counterproductive workplace behaviors, such as conflict, workplace bullying, and workplace violence, will be examined, along with practical approaches and tools for dealing with these issues. The discussion of motivational theories and their applications in the workplace will highlight techniques for boosting staff involvement in a safety-conscious environment. In addition, students will gain an understanding of the legislative requirements for safety, health, and well-being in the context of the UAE.
