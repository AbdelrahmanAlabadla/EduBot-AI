

# BACHELOR OF ARTS IN VIDEO GAME DESIGN

# Program Learning Outcomes

On successful completion of this program the graduate will be able to:

- PLO1: Analyze the historical and cultural evolution of video games, including genre development, key milestones, and contemporary trends, to understand their societal impact and influence on game design practices.
- PLO2: Design creative and innovative video game concepts, including mechanics, systems, and gameplay, while effectively addressing various constraints (artistic, technical, economic, etc.).
- PLO3: Create comprehensive and detailed documentation of the concepts, systems, processes, and organization required for the production or presentation of a video game.
- PLO4: Develop the gameplay of a video game by integrating and adjusting visual, audio, and interactive components within a professional game engine, showcasing advanced skills and knowledge in game design.
- PLO5: Communicate video game concepts effectively to different stakeholders, tailoring communication style and content to meet the specific needs and interests of each group.
- PLO6: Manage and oversee video game production while being able to thoroughly analyze data from operations and testing phases, to ensure optimal game quality at launch and throughout the live-ops phase.
- PLO7: Cultivate responsible and ethical professional skills based on an up-to-date understanding of the video game industry and its associated cultural dimensions, emphasizing continuous learning and adaptation.

# Program Mission

The Bachelor of Arts in Video Game Design at Abu Dhabi University, in collaboration with Rubika School (France), prepares students to become innovative game creators for the global industry. Combining art, design, programming, and serious applications, the program offers hands-on learning, industry collaborations, and international expertise. Students develop creative and technical skills to design immersive experiences while reflecting the UAE’s cultural heritage and digital vision. Graduates are equipped to contribute to game studios, startups, and emerging fields such as VR, AR, and simulation, supporting the UAE’s leadership in digital innovation.

# Program Goals

To reach the mentioned mission, the program has the following objectives:

- I. Develop a critical understanding of video games’ historical, cultural, and societal evolution, analyzing key milestones, genre development, and industry trends that shape contemporary game design and production.
- II. Develop expertise in designing creative and immersive video game experiences for diverse applications, including entertainment, education, simulation, and training, by integrating game mechanics, systems, and storytelling while effectively addressing artistic, technical, and economic constraints.
- III. Master industry-standard tools, game engines, software and production pipelines to create interactive and visually compelling gameplay, ensuring technical proficiency in game development.
- IV. Strengthen communication and collaboration skills by effectively presenting game concepts to diverse stakeholders, working in multidisciplinary teams, and managing production workflows in professional environments.
- V. Foster ethical responsibility, leadership, and adaptability by understanding industry best practices, engaging in continuous learning, and contributing to the evolving global landscape of video game development.
