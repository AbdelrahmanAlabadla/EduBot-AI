
College of Business Undergraduate Admission

1) Students who studied curriculum outside the Ministry of Education system in a language other than English can demonstrate proficiency by passing internationally standardized tests as determined by the university.

2) Students who studied curriculum outside the Ministry of Education system can prove proficiency in subjects relevant to their specialization as determined by the university.

3) Universities may grant conditional admission to students, provided they complete preparatory or remedial courses or programs offered by the university.

4) Foreign curriculum or curriculum which does not follow the UAE curriculum are required to submit an Equivalency Letter issued by the UAE Ministry of Education.

*** All students apply for College of Business are required to pass in a personal interview set by the College of Business.
