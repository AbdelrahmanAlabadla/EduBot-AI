# Major and Open Electives

15 Credit Hours

A student can choose one of the following combinations to satisfy the 15 credit hours of Electives:

- 3 courses (9 credit hours) from the Major Elective courses and 2 Free Elective courses (6 credit hours).
- 5 courses (15 credit hours) from the Major Elective courses.
