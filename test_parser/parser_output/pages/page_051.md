

# COLLEGE OF Arts, Education, and Social Sciences

# Introduction

The College of Arts, Education, and Social Sciences, one of the five colleges of Abu Dhabi University, offers courses in general education as well as courses leading to the award of Bachelor of Arts degrees. Through its diverse range of programs, the college offers academic research and practical courses that inspire and enhance the research and creative ability of the students. These courses provide students with both the oral and written communication skills, methods of scientific analysis and inquiry, and professional skills and ethics required for their future careers and undertakings. These components form the ethical and moral foundation for understanding, analyzing and resolving issues. These courses also prepare students for lifelong learning as well as for graduate studies in their areas of specialization.

Also, in addition to career designed programs in Mass Communication, CAESS offers you exciting new cutting-edge programs designed to prepare you for the demands of today’s dynamic job market and the evolving needs of a rapidly changing world: a BA in Video Game Design to bring your creative visions to life, a BA in International Relations to engage with global issues, a BA in Psychology to understand and enhance human interactions.

# Vision

To be a leading multidisciplinary college in the region, empowering students to become innovative thinkers, ethical leaders, and socially responsible citizens through transformative education in the arts, humanities, and social sciences.

# Mission

The College of Arts, Education and Social Sciences (CAESS) is committed to delivering high-quality, student-centered academic programs that blend creativity, critical thinking, and research with real-world application. Through its diverse offerings in communication, psychology, international relations, education, and digital technology, CAESS prepares graduates to meet the evolving demands of contemporary society and to contribute meaningfully to local and global communities.
