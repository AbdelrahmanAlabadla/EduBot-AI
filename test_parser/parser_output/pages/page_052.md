# College Objectives

The main objectives of the College of Arts, Education, and Social Sciences are:

1. To achieve academic and intellectual leadership by graduating students capable of original research and academic inquiry;
2. To prepare individuals capable of identifying and analyzing the interrelationships between programs in Creative Arts, and Social Sciences in the new age of interdisciplinary studies;
3. To develop an awareness of the needs of the learners and the society at large vis-à-vis ethical, professional, and socially responsible practices so as to meet the future needs of the region;
4. To equip students with the learning and research needs required for developing innovative endeavors and practices; and
5. To develop hands-on skills and competence in coping with the issues of individual and collective life-long decision-making.

# Mission

The mission of General Education is to help students successfully transition from high school to university life, by providing them with a comprehensive first year educational experience driven by retention and designed to equip them with essential skills for future success.
